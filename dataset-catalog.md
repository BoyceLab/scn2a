# References

Every standard, identifier system, vocabulary, and data source referenced across this site, with a link to its canonical home. The pages themselves link to these inline where each is first used; this page is the single consolidated list.

## Research information models

- [CERIF](https://eurocris.org/services/main-features-cerif), the Common European Research Information Format, maintained by [euroCRIS](https://eurocris.org/).
- [VIVO](https://vivoweb.org/), open-source semantic-web representation of scholarship.
- [PROV-O](https://www.w3.org/TR/prov-o/), the W3C provenance ontology.
- [RO-Crate](https://www.researchobject.org/ro-crate/), research object packaging.
- [schema.org](https://schema.org/), general structured-data vocabulary.

## Persistent identifiers

- [RAiD](https://raid.org/), Research Activity Identifier (ISO 23527:2022).
- [ORCID](https://orcid.org/), researcher identifiers.
- [ROR](https://ror.org/), Research Organization Registry.
- [DataCite](https://datacite.org/), DOIs for datasets and other outputs.
- [Crossref](https://www.crossref.org/), DOIs for publications, and the [Funder Registry](https://www.crossref.org/services/funder-registry/).
- [RRID](https://scicrunch.org/resources), Research Resource Identifiers via SciCrunch.
- [MANE](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/refseq/MANE/), Matched Annotation from NCBI and EMBL-EBI, for reference transcripts.

## Dataset catalog and interoperability

- [DCAT](https://www.w3.org/TR/vocab-dcat-3/), the W3C Data Catalog Vocabulary.
- [Bioschemas](https://bioschemas.org/), life-science profiles of schema.org.
- [HL7 FHIR](https://www.hl7.org/fhir/), including [ResearchStudy](https://www.hl7.org/fhir/researchstudy.html) and the [Genomics Reporting IG](https://hl7.org/fhir/uv/genomics-reporting/).

## Harmonization and vocabularies

- [OMOP CDM](https://ohdsi.github.io/CommonDataModel/) and the [OHDSI](https://www.ohdsi.org/) community, with vocabulary lookup via [Athena](https://athena.ohdsi.org/).
- [NINDS Common Data Elements](https://www.commondataelements.ninds.nih.gov/), including the [Epilepsy CDE set](https://www.commondataelements.ninds.nih.gov/Epilepsy).
- [ISO/IEC 11179](https://www.iso.org/search.html?q=11179), metadata registries.
- [GA4GH Phenopackets](https://www.ga4gh.org/product/phenopackets/) with the [Human Phenotype Ontology](https://hpo.jax.org/).
- [HGVS nomenclature](https://hgvs-nomenclature.org/) for variants.
- [GA4GH Variation Representation Specification](https://www.ga4gh.org/product/variation-representation-vrs/).
- [MONDO Disease Ontology](https://mondo.monarchinitiative.org/) and the [EBI Ontology Lookup Service](https://www.ebi.ac.uk/ols4/).

## Patient overlap and governance

- [GA4GH](https://www.ga4gh.org/), including the [Data Use Ontology](https://www.ga4gh.org/product/data-use-ontology-duo/).
- [Matchmaker Exchange](https://www.matchmakerexchange.org/) and [GA4GH Beacon](https://beacon-project.io/) for matching.
- [NIH GUID via FITBIR](https://fitbir.nih.gov/), one implementation of a global unique subject identifier.
- [Datavant](https://www.datavant.com/), commercial de-identified tokenization.

## Consortia and ecosystem

- [COMBINEDBrain](https://www.combinedbrain.org/), pre-competitive consortium of advocacy organizations.
- [RDCA-DAP](https://c-path.org/programs/rdca-dap/) at the [Critical Path Institute](https://c-path.org/).
- [CZI Rare As One](https://chanzuckerberg.com/rfa/rare-as-one/).
- [NCATS](https://ncats.nih.gov/) and [GARD](https://rarediseases.info.nih.gov/).

## Live data sources

- [PubMed](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/) and the [NCBI E-utilities](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK25501/).
- [Europe PMC](https://europepmc.org/) and its [REST API](https://europepmc.org/RestfulWebService).
- [NIH RePORTER](https://reporter.nih.gov/) and its [project API](https://api.reporter.nih.gov/).
- [ClinicalTrials.gov](https://clinicaltrials.gov/) and its [API v2](https://clinicaltrials.gov/data-api/api).
- [OpenAlex](https://openalex.org/).
